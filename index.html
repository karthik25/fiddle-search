Getting Started

I love jsfiddle the most among all other sites that offers the same functionality. But one thing I miss is the ability to search! That's why I came up with this! To get started, click on the icon, enter your user name and press the Save button. You will start seeing your fiddles & use the search text box to filter your fiddles! That's it!! Also, to hide the header image click on the icon. Have fun :)
